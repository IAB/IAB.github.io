* Music is an art form whose medium is sound. Its common elements are pitch (which governs melody and harmony), 

* Rhythm (and its associated concepts tempo, meter, and articulation),

* Dynamics, and the sonic qualities of timbre and texture.

* The creation, performance, significance, and even the definition of music vary according to culture and social context.

* Music ranges from strictly organized compositions (and their recreation in performance),

* Through improvisational music to aleatoric forms

* Music can be divided into genres and subgenres, although the dividing lines and relationships between music genres are often subtle,
